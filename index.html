    Frequently Asked Questions

    How frequently are long-term rental portable restrooms serviced?

    Weekly cleaning, pumping, and restocking are included in our regular service for long-term rentals. Depending on site requirements and consumption, more frequent service might be scheduled.

    Is it possible to rent a portable restroom in #State for just one day?

    For events or short-term projects, we do provide variable rental periods, including one-day rentals in #State. Get a price and talk about your unique demands by getting in touch with us.

    What distinguishes a premium portable toilet from a regular one?

    Basic amenities are offered by a typical portable toilet. For improved comfort and hygiene, a high-end portable toilet usually has a foot-pump flush system and a handwashing sink.

    Do you provide air-conditioned or heated portable toilets?

    For optimal comfort, our luxury restroom trailers frequently have climate control (heating and air conditioning), which makes them perfect for high-end events in a range of weather situations.

    Are your portable toilets in #State suitable for cold weather?

    Yes, many of our units are designed to operate effectively in various climates. For extreme cold, we can provide specialized units or winterization services to ensure functionality.
